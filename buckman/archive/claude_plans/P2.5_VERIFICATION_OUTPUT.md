## Validation Checks Implemented

### Hard Fails (Physics Violations)
1. **Negative pumping** — Cannot pump negative water
2. **Pumping > 3x historical max** — Engineering implausibility
3. **Depletion decreased** — Cumulative depletion must be monotonic

### Soft Flags (Statistical Outliers)
1. **Pumping outside 2σ** — Unusual but not impossible
2. **Pumping > 2x historical max** — Warrants human review
3. **Depletion > 1.5x historical max** — Growth faster than expected
